Katie is a huge music fan. In fact, she is a new talent scout for a prestigious record label. This summer, she travelled around the US looking for new groups to sign.

She saw The Keys in Nashville, Green Things in Austin, Peonies in Madison, and Black Cat in Seattle.

She was also supposed to see The Woodworks in Miami, but there was a hurricane and so the trip was cancelled at the last minute.

Her editor, Sam, is unsure that Katie went to Miami.

Sam tells a coworker, "Katie went to Nashville, Austin, Seattle, and Miami."


Sam knows where Katie went.
