Frannie is a painter. She just finished a new series of pieces, and wants to try showing them at a gallery. In an effort to make that happen, she goes around to different local galleries to speak with the curators.

She speaks to the curators at The Westside Gallery, Gallery L'enfant, and the Tick-Tock Gallery. She also planned to stop by the House of Art, but ran into a old friend on the way. They ended up grabbing coffee, so Frannie did not go to House of Art.

One of her models, Dustin, is sure that Frannie went to House of Art.


Dustin knows where Frannie went.
