Livia is a food journalist, and frequently has to travel to different cities for her job, interviewing chefs and tasting their culinary creations. She has four restaurants on her itinerary for a trip to New York City: Nina's, The Speckled Egg, Two for the Win, Scrumptuous, and Cloud 3. It turns out that Nina's has closed, so she doesn't go there.

Her partner, Melissa, thinks that Livia went to Nina's.

Melissa knows where Livia went.
